It's Manly Ferry Time! Why?

Why create such a simple app that only does one basic thing?

  • The ferry timetable is a serious constraint to your day/night in town/Manly
  • Missing the ferry sucks
  • I hate carrying paper timetables
  • I hate having to type in from/to into forms
  • I hate having to reload the page or redo the search
  • I don't want to see the arrival time, it confused me one night when I was drunk
  • I don't want to see the departure location, it's (almost) always the same place
  • I like simple clean websites
Disclaimer: information taken from published timetables, no responsibility taken for you missing the ferry.
